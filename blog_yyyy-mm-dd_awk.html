AWK for bioinformatics

Introduction, history and reason for writing post. Interestingly, awk was named after its developers by combining the first letter of their surnames; Aho, Weinberger & Kernighan. To get started using AWK, make sure to read the GNU AWK User's Guide, which is an excellent resource explaining the syntax of awk and showing its application through many examples.

Basics of AWK

The syntax of AWK might look daunting at first, but becomes very intuitive once you are familiar with its components. You can get started with simple commands to retrieve or edits text, and might even begin combining multiple search patterns or actions into a single awk command.

awk 'pattern {action}'

awk 'someword {print $0}' file.txt
